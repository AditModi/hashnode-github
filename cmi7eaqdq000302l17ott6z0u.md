## **Fixed-Rate Pricing Packages for Amazon CloudFront**

Think of this like a **fixed-price mobile data plan** instead of pay-per-GB.

Instead of being billed for every request, rule, lookup, or gigabyte, you‚Äôll be able to choose a **package** that includes the core edge components you need ‚Äî for **one predictable monthly price**.

### Why this matters:

* No surprise billing spikes from traffic surges
    
* No separate pricing math across multiple services
    
* **Simpler decision-making for both engineers and finance**
    

### The idea is straightforward:

You pick a plan.  
You pay the same amount **every month**.  
No overage charges.  
If you go beyond the included allowance, your traffic **still gets served** ‚Äî just potentially with reduced performance for the excess.
